Our foundation
Church Management Software Built to Help Churches Thrive
Amplify is a church management platform designed to empower 5 key pillars of a healthy church. Together, these pillars create shared experiences, strengthen connections, streamline processes, and establish a secure environment for worship — fostering a thriving community.
Pillar 1
Community & Discipleship
By investing in the spiritual growth of its members, the church can create a culture of discipleship that transforms lives.
Pillar 2
Communication & Engagement
Communication creates a sense of community and fosters a culture of trust, inclusion, and respect.
Pillar 3
Financial Stewardship
Build a culture of transparency and accountability, ensuring church funds are managed appropriately.
Pillar 4
Staff & Volunteer Effectiveness
Equip your team with the resources to carry out their roles effectively and without burnout.
Pillar 5
Safe Environments
Keep people safe with tools from background checks to real-time monitoring and secure check-in.
